Chow Mein Recipe

Chow mein is a chinese dish of stir fried noodles with mix veggies, soy sauce, and spices.

This dish is a popular choice for people who feel like eating fast food. This is a brilliant dish with which you can customize as much as you like.

Ingredients - for 2 serving

  • Water - 5 cups
  • Hakka noodles - 150/200gm
  • Cooking oil (refined or vegetable) - 2 teaspoon
  • veggies - all finely chopped
    • Onion - 2 of small size
    • Capsicum - 2 of small size
    • Carrot - 1 of medium size
    • Cabbage - 1/2 of medium size
  • Cornflour slurry - 2 teaspoon of cornflour with 2 tablespoon of water
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger-garlic paste
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Chilli sauce
  • Green chill sauce
  • Green chillies

Steps

  1. Boil the water add add 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  2. Add noodles, no need to break them.
  3. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  4. Cook noodles until tender and softened.
  5. Separate noodles from the water.
  6. Rinse the noodles very well under running water to stop the cooking process.
  7. Add cooking oil to the noodles.
  8. Toss them to cover the noodles well with oil well.
  9. Heat 3 tablespoon of oil in a pan on medium flames.Then, add the following ingredients:
    • Green chillies
    • ginger-garlic paste
  10. Fry green chillies and ginger-garlic paste for around 30 seconds.(Green chillies can be skipped if you don't like em.)
  11. Add 1/3 of chopped onions. Stir and fry for a minute.
  12. Add the following ingredients:
    • Grated carrots
    • Shredded cabbage
    • Thinly sliced capsicum
  13. Stir and increase flames to high. Stir fry everything on high flames for 3 minutes.
  14. Add 3 teaspoon of soy sauce, chilli sauce, green chilli sauce and vineger.(Also tomato sauce if you preferred.)
  15. Add 1/2 teaspoon black pepper.
  16. Add salt as required.
  17. Stir fry everything and then add the cornflour slurry. Make sure the slurry is mix well. Keep the flames low.
  18. Stir and fry for a minute on medium-low flames.
  19. Add noodles and increase the flames.
  20. Stir and quickly toss the noodles with the veggie mixture.
  21. Add 1/2 teaspoon rice vinegar and give a mix to chowmein.
  22. Serve it hot!
  23. May garnish it with 1 or 2 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds and some green onions.
